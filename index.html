KineticKit — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

KineticKit ("we", "us", "our") designs and sells running apparel. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Who we are

KineticKit is an independent apparel brand. Contact: kinetickitco@proton.me.

Information we collect

When you buy from our shop (via Etsy, our website, or other channels) or interact with our social accounts, we may receive: your name, shipping address, email address, order details, and basic analytics (such as pages viewed and referral source). Payment is processed by our sales platforms (e.g., Etsy, Shopify) — we do not store your full payment card details.

How we use it

  • To fulfil and ship your orders and provide customer support.
  • To send order updates and, if you opt in, occasional marketing emails (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • To understand which products and content resonate, so we can improve them.

Sharing

We share data only with providers that help us operate — such as our selling platforms, print and fulfilment partners, email tools, and analytics — and only as needed. We do not sell your personal information.

Social platforms

We use platforms such as Pinterest, Instagram, and TikTok to share our products. Their own privacy policies govern data collected on those platforms.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information by emailing kinetickitco@proton.me.

Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date will change accordingly.
